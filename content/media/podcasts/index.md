Artificial intelligence is no longer a distant technology of the future—it is already reshaping how people work, learn, create, and solve problems.
Artificial intelligence is often discussed in terms of productivity, jobs, and economic growth. But the deeper question is a human one: Will AI help us become more fully human?

In these episodes of The Economist Next Door, four different voices (yours truly was a participant!) share thoughts and opinions on ways to use AI and how it will impact human life.
Listen to these thought-provoking conversations on the economic and moral questions surrounding one of the most transformative technologies of our time.
